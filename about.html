About Me

Hi! I’m Sophia Bennett, a 24-year-old blogger from Toronto, Canada. I created this blog to help people understand personal finance in a clear, honest, and relatable way—without the jargon or fluff.

My passion for financial literacy started in college when I realized how little most of us are taught about managing money. Since then, I’ve spent years studying investing, budgeting, and wealth-building strategies.

This blog is a static website-just simple, straightforward knowledge. Every article is crafted with the intention of helping real people make smarter money decisions.

Thanks for stopping by. I hope you find value here, and if you do, feel free to share the blog with your friends!
