Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2025

This blog is a simple, static website created for educational purposes. We do not collect personal information from visitors, nor do we use cookies or third-party trackers.

1. No Data Collection

We do not require users to sign up, log in, or share any personal information to access our content. Your browsing activity is private and anonymous.

2. No Cookies or Analytics

This blog does not use cookies, Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or any form of user tracking. We respect your privacy and believe that educational content should not come at the cost of personal data.

3. External Links

Some posts may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., finance apps or books). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies if you choose to visit those sites.

4. Changes to This Policy

If this blog ever adds new features that involve data collection or cookies, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via the contact section (if available). Thanks for trusting us!
